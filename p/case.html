Dynamic Regenerative Integrated
Polymeric Skin (DRIPS)



Goal: Control moisture, heat, air, and radiation.

Idea: Use hygrothermal polymer membranes to respond to dynamic environmental conditions of relative humidity, dry-bulb temperature, solar radiation, natural ventilation, and water recuperation.
